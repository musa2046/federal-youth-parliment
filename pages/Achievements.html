Our Achievements

Celebrating the milestones and initiatives of the Federal Youth Parliament in empowering youth across Pakistan.

Achievements

Federal Youth Parliament (FYP) has made significant strides in youth empowerment and national development. FYP has organized 500+ events, conferences, and campaigns, engaging thousands of young individuals across Pakistan. It has established a presence in all provinces and major cities, creating a nationwide network of youth leaders.

Through its initiatives like the Green Movement, FYP launched plantation drives for sustainability. The Legal Forum has trained young lawyers through workshops and moot courts, while the Youth Parliament has promoted democracy and leadership. Women’s Forum has empowered women through education, training, and career programs.

These achievements reflect FYP’s commitment to building an informed, capable, and globally connected youth community dedicated to sustainable development.
