About Us

Federal Youth Parliament is a platform of young changemakers, committed to leadership, innovation, and creating lasting impact across Pakistan.

Who We Are

We are a youth-driven community passionate about empowering young voices and enabling them to become tomorrow’s leaders. Through collaborative efforts, educational programs, and impactful initiatives, we aim to shape a brighter and more inclusive future.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

  • To work with grass root level groups.
  • To work with all communities without discrimination for the development of target group.
  • Working on Rights based approach and issues.
  • Development of linkages with similar organisations.
  • Networking with other organisations working for the same cause.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide "Free of Charge" humanitarian services to society's differently abled and needy members, and to cure (treatment and rehabilitation) the helpless drug addicts and patients.

Organisation Objectives

We are committed to creating lasting impact through welfare, empowerment, and social development initiatives across Pakistan.

♿ Disability Welfare

Support and rehabilitation for people with disabilities, ensuring equal rights and development.

⚧ Transgender Rights

Working for dignity, inclusion, and equal opportunities for transgender communities.

✝ Minorities Welfare

Uplifting marginalized minorities through development, awareness, and support services.

🚭 Drug Abuse Prevention

Awareness programs, preventive education, and rehabilitation services for addicts.

👩‍👩‍👦 Women, Children & Youth

Programs to support growth, rights, and development through education and welfare.

📚 Education & Skills

Providing education, vocational training, water supply, and sanitation facilities.

🚫 Ending Harmful Practices

Eradication of child labor, women violence, and other harmful traditional practices.

🏥 Health & Awareness

Providing medical awareness, patient welfare, and rehabilitation for better health.

🦠 HIV/AIDS & Social Issues

Awareness, prevention, and services against HIV/AIDS, disasters, and social evils.

💪 Youth Empowerment

Building leadership, grooming political skills, and empowering youth to lead change.

⚖ Legislation Awareness

Educating youth on the legislation process to strengthen democracy and participation.
